Preview: How will The Lakers match up Against Boston?

John Peters | 27/01/2023 | 2 minute read

The Lakers will be starting their eastern conference road trip with a tough game against the reigning Eastern Conference champions Boston Celtics. The game will be played in Boston's home court so it will no doubt be a hostile crowd towards your Los Angeles Lakers.


They will be looking to avenge their fourth quarter collapse the last time these two teams played at the Lakers home court earlier in the season.


Jack Baer Yahoo Sports

The Celtics stole that game after the Lakers nearly made a huge comeback from 21 points down in the second quarter all the way to leading by 13 deep into the fourth.


With the Lakers trailing by only a few games out of not just a play in but a play off spot, this could be a huge win that would build upon the momentum they've been gathering over the past 4 games.


It will be a tough game without a doubt but the Lakers can be optimistic as it seems starting point guard Marcus Smart will miss the game due to an ankle injury.


"We're excited to have Anthony back, I think he's going to make a real difference for our team, and make us into the defensive team that we were earlier in the season. He won't be starting yet, but we're going to be a lot more lenient with his minutes restriction"
-Darvin Ham


The Lakers will be missing Austin Reaves, however Lonnie Walker the IV will be back after missing 2 weeks with knee tendonitis. It will be interesting to see how Ham adjusts the rotations as more and more players are getting healthy.
